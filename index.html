HOW TO BUY

Create a Wallet

Download Phantom Wallet or your preferred wallet from the App Store or Google Play Store for free. Phantom Wallet is strongly recommended.

Get SOLANA

Get some SOL in your Phantom Wallet to swap for $SBFJAILED . If you don't have any SOL, you can buy it directly on Phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your Phantom Wallet.

Buy on Raydium

Go to raydium.io in your regular browser or browser inside your Phantom Wallet. Connect your wallet. Paste the $SBFJAILED token adress into Raydium, select SBFJAILED, and confirm. When Phantom Wallet promps you for a signature, sign.

Swap SOL for $SBFJAILED

Swap SOL for $SBFJAILED . The token has zero taxes, so you don't need to worry about buying with high slippage. Although, you may need to use higher slippage during times of market volatility.
